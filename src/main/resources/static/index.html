Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car? ▼
To book your vehicle, all you need is a credit or debit card. When you pick up the vehicle, you'll need your voucher, the main driver's credit/debit card, their valid driving licence, and an additional form of ID (like a passport).
How old do I have to be to rent a car? ▼
For most rental companies, the minimum age requirement is between 21 and 70 years old. If you're under 25 or over 70, you might have to pay an additional fee.
Can I book a rental car for someone else? ▼
Yes, as long as they meet these requirements. Just fill in their details while you're making the reservation.
How do I find the cheapest car rental deal? ▼
We work with all the major international car hire brands to bring you a huge choice of cars at the very best prices. Just use our search form to compare deals.
What should I look for when I'm choosing a car? ▼
Consider the amount of luggage you'll have and the number of passengers. Make sure to check the fuel policy, mileage limits, and the amount required for the security deposit.
Are all fees included in the rental price? ▼
The vast majority of our rentals include Theft Protection, Collision Damage Waiver (CDW), local taxes, airport surcharges and any road fees. Any extra hidden fees will be clearly stated before booking.
