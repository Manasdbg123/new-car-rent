Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car? ▼
To book your vehicle, all you need is a credit or debit card. When you pick up the vehicle, you'll need your voucher, the main driver's credit/debit card, their valid driving licence, and an additional form of ID (like a passport).
How old do I have to be to rent a car? ▼
For most rental companies, the minimum age requirement is between 21 and 70 years old.
Are all fees included in the rental price? ▼
The vast majority of our rentals include Theft Protection, Collision Damage Waiver (CDW), local taxes, airport surcharges and any road fees. Any extra hidden fees will be clearly stated before booking.
